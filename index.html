About Us

Welcome to Hotel Lakeview.

Experience the pleasure of living beside Ooty lake with family and friends in the Queen of Hill station. The largest and the most exclusive hill resort in Ooty, Hotel Lakeview consists of 123 specially designed, fully independent luxury cottages. Hotel Lakeview is where you begin to experience Ooty.
Hotel Lakeview sits pretty on an 8 acre stretch of land, on the sensuous terraced slopes of a hill offering a magnificent double spectacle. Even at an ambling pace, you can reach the boathouse in 10 minutes flat, or the bus stand in 15 minutes.The complex has easy access to every part of the town and beyond.

Our Cottages & Facilities

Wht We Do

A charming & idyllic hideaway situated on the western slope of the Ooty Lake. Our spacious cottages offer guests a warm, welcoming, holiday sanctuary, perfect for your Hill experience. Our cottages are all independent, providing guests absolute privacy and a real feel of The Queen of Hill Stations.
With a choice of delectable dining options and comfortable accommodation, Hotel Lakeview is your place in Ooty. Offering Ooty’s largest Indoor and Outdoor Banquets and Conference facility, Hotel Lakeview is also the ideal location for weddings, family get-togethers, sales meetings, product launches, corporate hospitality and team building. With an array of other facilities and amenities we have lot more to offer to any kind of traveller.

See Ooty With Hotel Lakeview.

Welcome to Hotel Lakeview.

Ooty, Alluring, Vibrant, Tantalising, Mesmeric. Ooty Is Beyond Words. Ooty, Your Haven On The Hills! The joyful ride on 150-year-old Cog Wheel Heritage Train. The exciting experience of trout-fishing at the Avalanche Emerald. The daredevil winch ride down the sheer 3000 ft drop at Glen Morgan.The wide, wild world of animals, birds and reptiles at the Mudumalai Wildlife sanctuary. The chilling sight of the great Moyar Canyon. The spectacular vistas from the Valley View. The bursting colours and heady scents of Sim's Park. And then nature's mischievous handiworks at Dolphin's Nose and Lamb's Rock. In short, Ooty is one continuous stream of pure fun and pleasure!
