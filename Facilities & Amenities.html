Our Cottages

Each unit in the Lakeview complex is self-contained luxury cottage, with full-fledged amenities and facilities offering maximum privacy.

  • 450 Sq Ft Cottage
  • Private Garden In Front Of The Cottage
  • Open Deck
  • Bedroom
  • Living-Cum Dining Room
  • Queen Size Bed
  • Car Parking Facility

Dining

With a wide and tailor made menu to suit any kind of taste, Hotel Lakeview offers both In-room and Restaurant services that are perfect for relaxing meals with family and friends.

  • The Supper Club - serving you a choice of Continental, Chinese & Indian Cuisine.
  • Rooms Service- serving you a choice of Continental, Chinese & Indian.In-Room Dining is available from 6:30a.m. to 10:00pm.

Sight Seeing

  • The liquid beauty of the great Ooty lake
  • The kaleidoscopic beauty of the Botanical Gardens
  • The awesome view from Doddabetta, 3000 meters up in the sky
  • The joyful ride on 150 year old UNESCOCog Wheel Heritage Train
  • The exciting experience of trout-fishing at the Avalanche Emerald
  • The dare-devil winch ride down the sheer 3000 ft drop at Glen Morgan
  • The wide, wild world of animals, birds, and reptiles at the Mudumalai Wildlife Sanctuary
  • The chilling sight of the great Moyar Canyon.
  • The spectacular vistas from the Valley View.
  • The bursting colours and heady scents of Sim's Park.
  • And then nature's mischievous handiworks at Dolphin's Nose and Lamb's Rock.

In-House Activities

  • Horse Riding to experience Ooty and enjoy the freedom of riding out
  • Shops carrying books, periodicals, tourist requisites & souvenirs.
  • Table tennis, and other indoor/outdoor recreational facilities.
  • Bonfire , barbeque on request.
  • Separate children's play ground and picnic spots.
  • Trekking
